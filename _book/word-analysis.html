Analysis of Trip Advisor data relating to South Australian parks

7 Word analysis

7.1 Overall sentiment

Using the tidytext package (Robinson and Silge 2018), the text within the character fields Title and Review were analysed. Figure 7.1 shows the ten most common positive, negative and neutral words given in titles. Figure 7.2 shows the same for reviews.

The following words were reclassified as neutral from negative sentiment as they were overwhelmingly not used in a negative manner: cave; challenging; cold; crashing; desert; dirt; downhill; falls; miss; remarkable; rocky; ruins; shady; steep; unbelievable; unexpected; unusual; wedge; and wild.

The following words were removed from the analysis as they refer to park names or features and therefore occur frequently: adelaide; admirals; arch; breakaways; chase; coober; flinders; fur; granite; gully; harbor; harbour; island; kangaroo; ki; lofty; morialta; mount; mt; national; park; pedy; pound; ranges; remarkable; rocks; victor; waterfall; and wilpena.

Some words were also replaced with a synonym (Table 7.1).
